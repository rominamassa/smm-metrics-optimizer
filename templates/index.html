Organic Metrics

Metric Value Explanation
Reach The total number of unique users who saw your content.
Impressions The total number of times your content was displayed.
Followers The number of followers for your account.
Likes Total likes on your content.
Comments Total comments on your content.
Shares Total shares of your content.
Saves Total saves of your content.
Link Clicks Total link clicks on your content.

Results

  • Engagement Rate by Reach: (Engagements ÷ Reach) × 100
  • Engagement Rate by Impressions: (Engagements ÷ Impressions) × 100
  • Engagement Rate by Followers: (Engagements ÷ Followers) × 100
  • Discovery Rate: (Shares ÷ Reach) × 100
  • Click Rate: (Link Clicks ÷ Reach) × 100
