Organic Metrics

Metric Input Explanation
Reach Total unique users who saw your content
Impressions Total times your content was displayed
Followers Number of account followers
Likes Total likes on your content
Comments Total comments on your content
Shares Total shares of your content
Saves Total saves of your content
Link Clicks Total link clicks on your content

Results

  • Engagement Rate by Reach: ((Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves + Link Clicks) ÷ Reach) × 100
  • Engagement Rate by Impressions: ((Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves + Link Clicks) ÷ Impressions) × 100
  • Engagement Rate by Followers: ((Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves + Link Clicks) ÷ Followers) × 100
  • Discovery Rate: (Shares ÷ Reach) × 100
  • Click Rate: (Link Clicks ÷ Reach) × 100

Paid Metrics

Metric Input Explanation
Impressions Total ad/ad set/campaign impressions
Clicks Total clicks
Results Total results
Spend Total ad/ad set/campaign spend

Results

  • CTR: (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100
  • CPC: Spend ÷ Clicks
  • CPR: Spend ÷ Results
